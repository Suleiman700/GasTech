About Us

GasTech

Leading gas supply company with over 18 years of experience in importing and supplying gases to various industries, food industry, electronics, medical gases, and agriculture.

We provide all types of gases: carbon dioxide, argon, helium, cooking gas, medical gases, laboratory gases, and special mixtures.

Our company has a certified carbon dioxide filling station according to the United States standard.

We specialize in producing dry ice for use and storage in the pharmaceutical industry and also for the events and catering industry.

Our customers enjoy courteous, reliable, and professional service, while we tailor the product to each customer's real needs and preferences.
